Driving Rules: Comprehensive Guide

Master the art of driving safely and responsibly. Learn about essential rules and tips.

Basic Terms and Their Importance

Lane Discipline

Maintain your lane to ensure smooth traffic. Always use indicators when changing lanes.

Speed Limits

Adhering to speed limits prevents accidents and ensures safety for all road users.

Right of Way

Always yield to vehicles or pedestrians with the legal right to proceed first.

How to Navigate Common Scenarios

Merging Lanes

Check your mirrors, use indicators, and yield to ongoing traffic before merging.

Parking

Park in designated areas. Avoid obstructing traffic. Practice parallel parking.

Roundabouts

Yield to vehicles in the roundabout. Signal before exiting to indicate your turn.

Practical Tips and Advice

Plan your route using GPS or maps, especially in unfamiliar areas.

Keep a safe following distance to prevent sudden braking accidents.

Stay calm and focused, even during heavy traffic or road rage incidents.

Carry essential items like a first-aid kit, spare tire, and emergency contact numbers.
